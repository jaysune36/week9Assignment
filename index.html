Why Lizards are great pets?

Although some might think they are terrifying to look at, some lizards have some of the best and friendlist personalities. They are curious and love to be pet, with caution, and love to eat. They can be lazy and just love to sit in the sun all day without a care in the world. They can also be easy to talk to and if trained properly can be a great companion.
